About Us

We aimed to build an education tool that would recognize ASL Hand Shapes and translate their meanings to English

This would help: - Non-Signers trying to learn ASL - Signers trying to learn English - People who want to learn languages (Apps Like Duolingo) - Translation (in a pinch) for signers
